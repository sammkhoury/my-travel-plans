Day drinking because...KIDS.

Let's talk about day drinking. In this household a lot of alcohol is accesible but disclaimer, I do not drink. Not because I'm not against it or anything like that. We had our daughter is 2018 and could not stomach alcohol after the fact, so I just don’t drink. But anyways, I always have the thought pop up in my head that I need a drink by the evening (some days sooner than that) & I see the beers, seltzers, liquor, all that available but I just don’t commit because I don’t want to feel horrible, knowing I will. But I definitely get to that stress point of like oh my god I need a drink. So I want to know how people feel about that? I am a stay at home mom and if I did drink, let it be that nothing is going on and we are not leaving the house for the day, I’m definitely cracking a few and getting a good buzz. So for any of you out there that have kids and get to that point of needing a drink, do you drink around your kids when you are the supervisory parent? I’m not talking social hour with friends over, I mean just it’s been A DAY and the kids are making you lose your shiitake mushroom. Even though it’s just you and them, let’s be for real — it’s time for a drink.

Sincerely, The Humble Bee
