Elephant

Elephants are highly social and intelligent creatures, and they demonstrate behaviors we humans recognize as compassion, kindness, and altruism.

  • Average Size: 6.5 – 13 ft
  • Average Weight: 6,600 – 15,500 lbs
  • Top Speed: 15.5 Mph (30 Kph)
  • Diet: Grass, plants, fruits, twigs, tree bark, roots

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks. They use their trunks to pick up objects, trumpet warnings, greet other elephants, or suck up water for drinking or bathing, among other uses.
